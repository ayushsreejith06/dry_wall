## ðŸ”§ Test via PowerShell Terminal

```powershell
# Get robot status
Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "http://localhost:8000/status" -Method Get | ConvertFrom-Json | ConvertTo-Json

# Move forward (speed 0.5)
Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "http://localhost:8000/move" -Method Post `
  -Headers @{"Content-Type"="application/json"} `
  -Body '{"speed": 0.5}' | ConvertFrom-Json | ConvertTo-Json

# Turn left (speed 0.3)
Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "http://localhost:8000/turn" -Method Post `
  -Headers @{"Content-Type"="application/json"} `
  -Body '{"speed": 0.3, "direction": "left"}' | ConvertFrom-Json | ConvertTo-Json

# Stop
Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "http://localhost:8000/stop" -Method Post `
  -Headers @{"Content-Type"="application/json"} `
  -Body '{}' | ConvertFrom-Json | ConvertTo-Json
```
